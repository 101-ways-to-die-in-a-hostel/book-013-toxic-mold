# Victim Profile

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Sage Moonriver (born: Jennifer Kowalski)
**Nickname/Handle:** @SageLivingLight
**Age:** 29
**Nationality:** American (Oregon, originally Wisconsin)
**Occupation:** Wellness influencer, "holistic health coach"

## Archetype
**Primary:** Spiritual Tourist / Wellness Zealot

**Secondary traits:** Medical denier, crystal enthusiast, "natural living" extremist

## The Fatal Flaw
**Core Flaw:** Hubris (believing her "high vibration" and "clean living" make her immune to physical reality)

**How it manifests:**
- Refuses all pharmaceutical medicine, vaccines, and modern medical intervention
- Attributes all illness to "low vibration" or "spiritual blockages"
- Believes her body will "heal itself" if she maintains proper diet and mindset
- Dismisses symptoms as "detox" or "healing crises"

**How it kills them:**
She ignores weeks of respiratory symptoms from mold exposure, treating them with essential oils and meditation instead of medical care, until her lungs are irreversibly damaged.

## Physical Appearance
- **Height/Build:** 5'6", willowy, increasingly gaunt as illness progresses
- **Hair:** Sun-bleached blonde, usually in elaborate braids with crystals woven in
- **Distinguishing features:** Multiple chakra tattoos, always barefoot, permanent "serene" expression
- **Typical outfit:** Flowing organic cotton, mala beads, at least three crystals somewhere on her person

## Personality

### Positive Traits (that get twisted)
1. Self-aware - becomes narcissistic navel-gazing
2. Health-conscious - becomes health-obsessed to the point of denying actual illness

### Negative Traits (that seal their fate)
1. Arrogant certainty - believes she knows better than doctors, science, reality
2. Toxic positivity - refuses to acknowledge anything negative, including danger signs

### Pet Peeves
- "Western medicine" and "Big Pharma"
- People with "low vibrations" (ironic: she'll die surrounded by mold spores)

### Fears
- Surface fear: Pharmaceutical dependency, "toxins"
- Deeper fear: Being ordinary, being Jennifer Kowalski from Appleton, Wisconsin

## Background

### Origin Story
Jennifer grew up in a working-class Wisconsin family, watched her grandmother suffer through aggressive cancer treatment, and concluded that medicine was worse than disease. She moved to Oregon after college, discovered yoga, renamed herself Sage, and built an online following by documenting her "clean living" journey. She genuinely believes she's helping people. She's never been seriously ill—until now.

### Why They Travel
To deepen her spiritual practice at "high energy" locations. To gather content for her followers. To prove that her lifestyle can flourish anywhere. Running from: her ordinary origins. Seeking: validation that her choices are right.

### How They Ended Up at This Hostel
Reviews mentioned "natural," "eco," and "spiritual energy." The basement dorm was cheapest and available during a popular festival week. Sage chose it specifically because it was below ground level—"closer to Earth's healing energy."

## Voice & Speech

### Vocabulary
- Uses words like: "vibration," "alignment," "toxic," "natural," "healing crisis," "detox"
- Avoids words like: "doctor," "medicine," "sick" (prefers "experiencing symptoms")
- Catchphrases: "Trust the process," "Your body knows what it needs," "I'm just detoxing"

### Speech Pattern
- Breathy, measured delivery
- Lots of pauses for "centering"

### Internal Monologue Style
Constant self-reassurance. Running commentary of affirmations. Whenever a worried thought appears, she immediately counters it with wellness speak. The internal voice is exhausting in its relentless positivity.
