# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 013
**Title:** Toxic Mold

## The Death (One Sentence)
A wellness influencer who refuses modern medicine suffers progressive respiratory failure from Stachybotrys (black mold) exposure in a damp basement dorm, dying after weeks of ignoring her body's warnings.

## Tagline
"Some things can't be cleansed with positive thinking."

## Themes
1. The dangerous intersection of wellness culture and medical denial
2. How positive thinking can become toxic positivity
3. The slow violence of environmental hazards in budget accommodations

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 30%
- Horror: 45%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's rolled their eyes at "natural immunity" culture, travelers who've slept in suspiciously damp rooms, readers who enjoy watching willful ignorance lead to consequences.

## Comparison Titles
